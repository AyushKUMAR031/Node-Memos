Uchiha Clan Characters

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is one of the last surviving members of the Uchiha clan. He possesses the Sharingan and later awakens the Mangekyou Sharingan. Sasuke is known for his exceptional talent in ninjutsu and taijutsu.

Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha was a prodigy of the Uchiha clan and possessed the Sharingan from a young age. Despite his calm and composed demeanor, Itachi was a master tactician and a formidable opponent in battle.

Madara Uchiha

Madara Uchiha was one of the most powerful and legendary shinobi in history. He was the founder of the Uchiha clan and possessed the Eternal Mangekyou Sharingan. Madara was known for his immense chakra and mastery of various jutsu.

Obito Uchiha

Obito Uchiha, also known as Tobi, was a former member of Team Minato and later became a major antagonist in the Naruto series. He wielded the Sharingan and later obtained the Rinnegan. Obito was a skilled manipulator and possessed immense power.

Shisui Uchiha

Shisui Uchiha was a highly talented member of the Uchiha clan known as "Shisui of the Body Flicker." He possessed the Kotoamatsukami, a genjutsu that allowed him to manipulate others without them realizing it. Shisui was admired for his selflessness and loyalty to the clan.
